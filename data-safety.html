Data Safety

Transparency on how ReportX collects, processes, and protects your data.

Overview

ReportX is designed with your privacy in mind. We follow the Google Play Data Safety guidelines to explain clearly what data is collected, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Data We Collect

  • Email Address: Required for login and account creation via Firebase Authentication.
  • Lab Report Images & Extracted Data: Captured via device camera or selected from gallery for AI analysis.
  • App Usage & Device Data: Crash logs, performance metrics, and analytics events.

How Your Data is Used

  • To authenticate and manage your account (using email address).
  • To provide AI-powered health insights from your lab reports (Gemini API processing).
  • To store and display lab reports locally on your device.
  • To improve app stability and fix crashes (Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics).

Data Sharing

We only share limited data with trusted third parties as needed for app functionality:

  • Google Gemini API: When you request an analysis, the lab report image/text is sent temporarily for processing.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Collect usage, crash, and device data to improve the app.

We do not sell your data to advertisers or share it for marketing purposes.

Data Security

Sensitive health data (lab reports) is stored only on your device unless you choose to export it. Data transferred to Gemini API is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

Data Retention

Locally stored data: Remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
Account data: You may delete your account anytime in the profile section; this will erase your Firebase Auth account and associated metadata.
Analytics/Crash data: Retained by Firebase for product improvement only.

Your Rights

  • You can delete reports inside the app.
  • You can delete your account (and email) anytime from your profile page.
  • You can uninstall the app to erase all locally stored lab reports.
  • You can contact us for any privacy-related request.
