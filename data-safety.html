Data Safety

Transparency on how ReportX collects, uses, and protects your data.

Overview

ReportX is designed with your privacy and safety in mind. We follow the Google Play Data Safety guidelines to ensure you understand what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Data We Collect

  • Lab Report Images: Captured using your device camera or uploaded from storage.
  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics (e.g., crash logs, performance) to improve functionality.

No sensitive personal identifiers (such as your name, email, or phone number) are required to use the app.

How Your Data is Used

  • To provide AI-powered health insights from your lab reports.
  • To store your lab reports securely on your device for later access.
  • To improve app performance and reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared when:

  • Required by law or government authorities.
  • With your explicit consent (e.g., exporting reports).

Data Security

All data is stored securely on your device. When cloud backup is enabled, reports are encrypted before storage. We implement strong technical safeguards to prevent unauthorized access.

Data Retention

Your lab reports remain stored locally in the app until you delete them. If you uninstall ReportX, all stored data is permanently removed from your device.

Your Rights

  • You can delete lab reports at any time within the app.
  • You can uninstall the app to erase all locally stored data.
  • You can contact us for any privacy-related requests.
