Refund Policy

Effective date: September 17, 2025

Eligibility for Refunds

Purchases made through Google Play Store or Apple App Store are subject to the respective store’s Google Play refund policy and Apple Media Services Terms & Conditions.

Because all transactions are handled by these platforms, we cannot process refunds directly for purchases made through them.

Subscriptions & Cancellations

If you purchase a subscription in ReportX:

  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Google Play or Apple App Store account settings.
  • Cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle; you will retain access until then.
  • Partial refunds for unused time within a billing cycle are generally not provided, unless required by law or the app store’s policies.

Exceptions

Refunds may be granted in special cases, such as:

  • Duplicate purchases due to technical errors.
  • Unauthorized transactions (if promptly reported to the app store and to us).
  • Legal requirements in certain jurisdictions that mandate refunds.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please use the official channels:

  • Google Play: Visit Play Store Order History and select “Request a Refund.”
  • Apple App Store: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow the steps.

For questions, you may also contact our support team at drktvtech@gmail.com. While we cannot override Play or Apple refund decisions, we will assist you in the process.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Material changes will also be communicated in-app where appropriate.
