Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ReportX (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services (the “App”).

1. Overview and Medical Disclaimer

ReportX helps users scan and understand clinical lab report pages using on-device and cloud-based AI.

ReportX is not a healthcare provider and does not provide medical diagnosis. The App provides informational AI explanations based on lab report text and values. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice and before making medical decisions.

2. Data Collection and Storage

A. Information You Provide

  • Images of Lab Reports: Required for the App’s analysis feature.
  • Account Information: If you sign up or log in, we collect your email address (and password, managed securely by Firebase Authentication).
  • Support Messages: If you contact support, we collect the content you provide and any attachments.

B. Storage and Local Processing

Lab report data, scanned images, and analysis history are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to our servers. Deleting the app or clearing its data deletes this information.

C. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Events like app version, feature usage, taps (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Device & Performance Data: Device model, OS version, performance metrics, crash logs (via Crashlytics).
  • Identifiers: Device IDs for linking crash reports and analytics.

3. Data Usage and Processing

How we use information

4. Third-Party Services and Sharing

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages accounts and securely stores email/password credentials.
  • Google Gemini API: Processes lab report images/text for AI analysis.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Collects anonymous usage, performance, and crash data.

We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Security

Lab report data is stored only on your device. Data sent to Firebase and Gemini is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

6. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains until deleted by you. Firebase Authentication retains your account until you request deletion. Analytics/crash logs are kept as per Firebase retention policies.

7. Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: You can view and update your account details (such as email) from your profile page in the App.
  • Account Deletion: You may delete your account directly from the User Profile page in the App. This will permanently remove your authentication details (email and login) from Firebase Authentication. Locally stored lab reports will remain on your device unless you uninstall the App or clear its data.
  • Data Deletion: To delete lab reports and analysis history, you can manually delete them within the App or uninstall the App.
  • Opting out of analytics: You may opt out of non-essential analytics at the OS level.

    • Account Data: Your email/login is retained as long as you have an account. If you delete your account via the profile page, your authentication data is permanently deleted from Firebase Authentication.

8. Children

Not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
